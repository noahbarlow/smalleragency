supa power.
brand · packaging · e-commerce · content · paid
01the objective

bold, unboring, built for performance.

The supplement aisle is a wall of black tubs and shouting fonts. The goal was a brand that stood out in a crowded industry and engaged high-performance athletes — disruptive branding, edgy content, and a direct-to-consumer platform.

We broke from traditional, uninspired supplement marketing: high-energy visuals, flavor-forward messaging, and a streamlined shopping experience.

02what we shipped

the full stack.

Brand → Package → Demand, run end to end on one engagement. Every deliverable feeds the next — nothing shipped as a one-off.

  • brand & asset library + brand guide — social, paid & online
  • influencer & affiliate playbook
  • sell-sheet & catalog
  • e-commerce (shopify)
  • email flows — subscribers, abandoned cart, reviews
03creative execution

from name to nationwide.

strategy & naming

Developed the SUPA POWER name and brand essence — bold, unboring, and built for serious performance.

identity & packaging

High-impact packaging that stands out in the crowded supplement space — urban culture, energetic aesthetics, flavor first.

tone of voice

A distinct, no-BS personality that speaks directly to high-performing fitness communities.

shopify buildout

Designed and launched supa-power.com — a sleek, high-converting e-commerce site optimized for seamless shopping.

photography & content

A full suite of product shots, lifestyle imagery and motion assets to fuel every channel that follows.

instagram launch

Launched the SUPA POWER account with bold, high-energy content that separated it from every other supplement feed.

influencers & creators

Macro, micro and nano creators across CrossFit, bodybuilding, MMA and Olympic lifting.

paid media

A nationwide Instagram campaign built for conversion, targeting high-performance fitness enthusiasts and supplement buyers.
